Frequently Asked Questions

How will my donation be used?
Your donation supports our community programs, cultural events, youth initiatives, and essential services for Congolese families in Worcester. We maintain transparency with annual reports available to all donors.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, CCWC is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.
Can I set up recurring donations?
Absolutely! We offer monthly and yearly recurring donation options. Recurring donations help us plan our programs more effectively and provide sustained support to our community.
What other ways can I support CCWC?
Beyond financial donations, we welcome volunteers, in-kind donations, and corporate sponsorships. You can also support us by attending our events and promoting our work in your networks.
