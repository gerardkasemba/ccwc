Volunteer With CCWC

Join our dedicated team of volunteers and make a difference in the Congolese community of Worcester

Why Volunteer With Us?

Make a Real Impact

Your time and skills directly support our community members and help preserve our cultural heritage.

Build Connections

Meet like-minded individuals, form lasting friendships, and expand your professional network.

Gain Valuable Experience

Develop new skills, enhance your resume, and gain experience in community organization and leadership.

Personal Fulfillment

Experience the joy of giving back and being part of positive change in your community.

Volunteer Opportunities

Youth Program Assistant

4-8 hours/week • Weekends

Help with our after-school programs, tutoring sessions, and cultural education activities for children and teenagers.

  • Assist with homework help
  • Lead cultural activities
  • Organize educational games

Event Support

Flexible hours • Evenings/Weekends

Help organize and run our community events, cultural celebrations, and fundraising activities.

  • Event setup and coordination
  • Food preparation and service
  • Guest registration

Digital Media Assistant

2-5 hours/week • Remote/Flexible

Help manage our social media, create content, and maintain our website to keep the community informed.

  • Social media management
  • Content creation
  • Newsletter preparation

Community Outreach

3-6 hours/week • Flexible

Connect with new community members, share information about our services, and help with resource distribution.

  • Welcome new families
  • Distribute resources
  • Community surveys

Facility Maintenance

Flexible hours • Weekdays

Help maintain our community center, organize storage, and assist with minor repairs and improvements.

  • Light maintenance tasks
  • Organization projects
  • Setup for activities

Translation Services

As needed • Remote/Flexible

Help translate documents, assist community members with interpretation, and support communication efforts.

  • Document translation
  • Interpretation assistance
  • Bilingual support
