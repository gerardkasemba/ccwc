Our Operational Structure

Executive Director

Oversees all organizational operations and implements strategic plans

Programs Manager

Develops and manages community programs and initiatives

Finance Officer

Manages budgeting, financial reporting, and fiscal operations

Communications Coordinator

Handles public relations, marketing, and community outreach

Membership Coordinator

Manages member services, recruitment, and engagement

Events Coordinator

Plans and executes community events and activities

Our Operational Mission

The Executive Committee is dedicated to implementing the strategic vision set by the Board of Directors. We work tirelessly to ensure the smooth operation of CCWC, delivering programs and services that enrich the lives of our community members while preserving and promoting Congolese culture in Worcester.
