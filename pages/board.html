Board of Directors

Meet the dedicated leaders guiding the Congolese Community of Worcester

Our Leadership Structure

Chairperson

Provides overall leadership and strategic direction for the organization

Treasurer

Manages financial resources and ensures fiscal responsibility

Secretary

Maintains records and ensures effective communication

Board Members

Provide governance, oversight, and strategic guidance

Meet Our Board Members

Our diverse board brings together professionals from various fields who are committed to serving the Congolese community in Worcester.

Jean-Luc Mbala

Chairperson

Our Guiding Mission

The Board of Directors is committed to advancing the mission of the Congolese Community of Worcester by providing strategic leadership, ensuring financial sustainability, and upholding our values of unity, cultural preservation, and community empowerment.

Contact the Board

For general inquiries or to contact the Board of Directors:

Email: board@ccwc.org

Phone: (508) 123-4567
