How Our Service Exchange Works

The CCWC Services platform connects community members who have skills to offer with those who need assistance. Whether you're looking for help or want to contribute to our community, this is the place to connect.

All services are exchanged within our community to strengthen bonds and support one another.

To get started:

  • Browse available services below
  • Use the filters to find specific services
  • Contact service providers directly
  • Offer your own skills to help others
