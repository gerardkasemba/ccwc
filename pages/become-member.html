Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a member of CCWC?
Anyone with Congolese heritage or connection to the Congolese community in Worcester is welcome to join. We also welcome allies who support our mission.
What is the membership fee used for?
Membership fees help support our community programs, events, administrative costs, and resources that benefit all members. We maintain financial transparency with annual reports available to all members.
Can I volunteer instead of paying the membership fee?
While we welcome and appreciate volunteers, membership fees are essential for sustaining our organization. However, we do offer limited scholarship memberships for those facing financial hardship.
How do I renew my membership?
We send renewal reminders via email 30 days before your membership expires. You can renew online, by mail, or in person at our office.
